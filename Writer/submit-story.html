Submission Guidelines

  • Word Count: Short fiction (1,000-5,000 words), Flash fiction (under 1,000 words), Poetry (no more than 3 poems per submission).
  • Content: We welcome stories in all genres, but they should be appropriate for a general audience.
  • Format: Please submit your work in plain text. Basic HTML formatting is allowed.
  • Rights: By submitting, you retain all rights to your work. We request non-exclusive rights to publish it on our website.
  • Response Time: We aim to respond to all submissions within 2-4 weeks.
  • Multiple Submissions: Please submit only one story at a time and wait for a response before submitting another.
